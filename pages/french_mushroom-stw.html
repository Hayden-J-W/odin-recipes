French Mushroom Stew

Return Home

Description

n this recipe, mushrooms are simmered with onions, wine, and carrots, making for a rich, French-style mushroom stew. Serve it with egg noodles, polenta, or mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound cremini or white mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 large carrots, sliced
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add chopped onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  2. Add sliced mushrooms and carrots to the pot. Cook until the mushrooms release their moisture and start to brown, about 10 minutes.
  3. Pour in vegetable broth and white wine. Stir in dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and let simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the carrots are tender and the stew has thickened slightly.
  4. Adjust seasoning if needed. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley before serving, if desired. Enjoy!
